

# Portfolio Website for Thajmal Khan

A modern, clean corporate-style portfolio website showcasing AI development skills and creative expertise.

## Design System
- **Colors**: White background, Navy Blue (#1e3a5f) primary, Steel Blue (#4682b4) accent, Charcoal (#2d2d2d) text, Light Gray (#f5f5f5) sections
- **Font**: Inter (clean, professional)
- **Style**: Minimalistic, card-based, subtle fade-in animations, smooth scrolling

## Pages & Sections (Single-page layout)

### 1. Sticky Navigation Bar
- Logo with "TK" initials
- Links: Home, About, Skills, Services, Projects, Experience, Contact
- Mobile hamburger menu

### 2. Hero Section
- Split layout: intro text left, placeholder profile photo right
- Name, title ("AI Student | Video Editor | Creative Technologist"), short bio
- Two CTA buttons: "View Projects" and "Contact Me"
- Subtle animated abstract AI-inspired background lines

### 3. About Me
- Two-column layout with bio text and education timeline
- Icon highlights for AI, Video Editing, Digital Marketing

### 4. Skills Section
- Three category cards: Software & Tools, Creative Skills, Technical Skills
- Minimal icon grid with subtle hover effects

### 5. Services Section
- Service cards with icons for Video Editing Services and AI Automation Projects
- Shadow and hover elevation effects

### 6. Projects Section
- Featured project card for Voice Assistant Project
- Technical breakdown (tools used, features)
- GitHub link button

### 7. Experience Section
- Corporate vertical timeline
- Video Editor Intern at Plant Green Inertia with bullet points

### 8. Contact Section
- Contact info (email, phone, location) with icons
- Social links (LinkedIn, GitHub, Instagram)
- Contact form (Name, Email, Message) — frontend only, no backend
- Footer with © 2026 Thajmal Khan and social icons

## Key Features
- Fully responsive (mobile-first)
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Fade-in animations on scroll
- Professional "TK" favicon
- All content from the provided details hardcoded in
